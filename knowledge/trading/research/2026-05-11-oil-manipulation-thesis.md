# Oil Price Manipulation Thesis — Paper vs Physical Divergence
**Created:** 2026-05-11
**Status:** Active thesis, monitoring

## Core Thesis
Oil futures (paper price) are being artificially suppressed ~$40 below physical delivery prices. This gap CANNOT persist and will close — most likely via violent upward repricing of futures when inventory buffers are exhausted.

## Evidence

### Paper vs Physical Gap
- Paper futures (WTI/Brent): ~$100/bbl
- Physical delivery prices: $130-$144+/bbl
- Gap: $30-$40/bbl (confirmed by Al Jazeera, Investing.com, EBC, Marketplace, Energy News Beat)
- Futures dominated by hedge funds/algo traders betting disruption is temporary
- Physical buyers paying massive premium for immediate delivery

### Inventory Depletion (Critical)
- Morgan Stanley: global stockpiles dropping 4.8M barrels/DAY (Mar 1 - Apr 25)
- Fortune/Bloomberg (May 9, 2026): "Iran war draining world's oil buffer at unprecedented pace"
- Asia-Pacific inventories (ex-China) down 70M barrels since conflict
- US SPR at 413M barrels (historic lows) BEFORE crisis
- IEA released 400M barrels from strategic reserves — insufficient
- Chevron CFO Eimear Bonner: "A lot of inventory and spare capacity has been DEPLETED ALREADY"
- Critical shortage window: **June-July 2026**

### Supply Shock Scale
- 12M barrels/day removed from global supply (12% of total production)
- Largest oil supply shock in history (exceeds 1973 + 1979 combined per IEA)
- Drawable reserves estimated 800M-2.5B barrels
- At current drawdown: 1.2B barrels depleted every 100 days
