## KEY LEARNINGS

1. **Options as Leading Indicator**: When options extremely bullish/bearish (>80%) and technical weak, options often lead price. See NVDA (bullish) and EQIX (bearish).

2. **Heavy Put Buying on Rallies = Warning**: EOG showing this pattern. When price rallying but puts being accumulated, suggests hedging or top formation.

3. **Technical Bearish + Options Bullish at Support = Potential Reversal**: JNJ and MCD showing this. Often occurs at major support levels. High risk but can be profitable if confirmed.

4. **Timeframe Divergence**: 4H vs Daily divergence (AAPL) is normal and creates entry opportunities. This is different from technical vs options divergence.

5. **Volume Divergence**: HPE shows price making new highs on declining volume - classic warning sign even if technicals bullish.

6. **MACD vs SMA Conflicts**: When MACD bearish but SMA bullish (or vice versa), price usually in consolidation. Wait for breakout direction. See MSFT, NVDA, EQIX.

## RISK MANAGEMENT RULES

Based on discrepancies found:

1. **NO POSITION** when technical and options conflict >60% strength difference (EQIX, EOG)
