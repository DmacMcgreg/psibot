**Verdict:** Selective stock-picking essential. Avoid index-level exposure; focus on quality (GOOGL, AAPL) with proven earnings power.

- Fed expected to cut 2 times in 2026
- Target range: 3.25-3.50% by year-end
- Approach: "Wait-and-see" vs. aggressive easing

**Verdict:** Mild positive for tech from rate cuts, but not enough to justify bubble valuations. Focus on companies with pricing power (AAPL, GOOGL) that can handle inflation.

**Meta:**

- Risk: Low (earnings-related)

**Verdict:** GOOGL faces highest regulatory risk with AdX divestiture. Could unlock value (AdX separate) or impair margins. Amazon trial in late 2026 is wild card. Microsoft/Meta lower risk.

**FCF Performance:**
- **Amazon:** $1.2B (down from $25.9B) - DISASTER

**Verdict:**
- **Concern:** AMZN, META (FCF collapse without clear revenue acceleration)
